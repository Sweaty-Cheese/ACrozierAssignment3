What do I Need to do to Prepare For My Treatment?

All treatments are done FULLY CLOTHED.
Please dress comfortably.
If you are not wearing socks, please bring some. This is because I use lotions and this will help the lotions to soak into your feet.
Wear pants that are loose and can roll up to your knees.
Please be sure to not come on an empty stomach, but not a super full one either.
Please do not drink or take drugs before your appointment.
